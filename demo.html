Why choose self-hosted over an API?

Complete control over data privacy, zero latency for internal apps, and no recurring per-email costs. You own the infrastructure.

Not at all. Our Docker-based deployment allows you to get a production-ready instance running in under 5 minutes.

How accurate is the verification?

We maintain 99.9% accuracy using an 8-step deep check process including SMTP handshakes and real-time DNS validation.

Do you support custom integrations?

Yes. Beyond our native connectors, you can build custom workflows using our flexible webhooks and automation engine.
