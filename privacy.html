Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

1. Introduction

CleanMails ("we", "our", or "us") operates the website cleanmails.online. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our email verification services.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Name and email address when you create an account
  • Billing information and payment details for subscription services
  • Company name and job title (optional)

Usage Data

  • IP address, browser type, and device information
  • Pages visited, time spent, and navigation patterns
  • API usage logs and verification request metadata

Email Data

  • Email addresses submitted for verification (processed and not stored permanently)
  • Verification results and bounce data

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our email verification service
  • To process transactions and send billing notifications
  • To communicate with you about service updates and support
  • To improve our platform performance and user experience
  • To detect and prevent fraudulent activity
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to provide our services. Email addresses submitted for verification are processed in real-time and are not stored beyond the verification session unless explicitly saved by the user. Account data is retained until you request deletion.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Payment processors to handle transactions securely
  • Cloud infrastructure providers for hosting services
  • Analytics services to improve our platform (anonymized data only)
  • Law enforcement when required by law

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (TLS 1.3), encryption at rest, regular security audits, and strict access controls. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and receive a copy of your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data
  • Data portability

8. Cookies

We use essential cookies to maintain your session and preferences. Analytics cookies are used only with your consent to help us understand how visitors interact with our platform.
