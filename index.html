FAQ

Common questions.

CleanMails is a self-hosted email verification tool. You install it on your own VPS, upload email lists or use the API, and it performs 8-step deep verification — syntax, MX, DNS, SMTP handshake, catch-all, disposable, role-based, and free-mail detection. Your data never leaves your server.

One command. Run the install script with your license key and you're live in under 3 minutes. It auto-installs Docker, PostgreSQL, Redis, sets up SSL, and creates your admin account — no manual configuration needed.

No limits at all. Once you purchase CleanMails, you can verify unlimited emails — single, bulk CSV (100K+ at a time), or via API. There are no per-email costs and no monthly quotas. It runs on your hardware, so you scale as much as your server allows.

Any Linux VPS with 1GB RAM, 1 CPU core, and port 25 open for SMTP checks. Ubuntu 22.04+ or Debian 12+ recommended. A $5–10/month VPS from providers like Hetzner, DigitalOcean, or Contabo works perfectly.

Native integrations: HubSpot, Mailchimp, Brevo, Mailgun, and Resend. Automation platforms: Zapier, n8n, and Make (connecting you to 5,000+ apps). AI/MCP: works with Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, Cursor, and Kiro. Plus a full REST API, webhooks, and TypeScript/Python SDKs for custom scripts.

99.4% bounce reduction using real SMTP handshakes — not just DNS lookups. We verify the mailbox actually exists at the server level. Plus catch-all detection with a triple-probe algorithm, and a database of 123,000+ disposable domains updated daily.

CleanMails runs entirely on YOUR server. We have zero access to your data — no cloud sync, no telemetry, no third-party calls. Email lists stay in your PostgreSQL database and nowhere else. You own 100% of your data.

Yes — lifetime updates are included with your one-time purchase. New features, security patches, disposable domain list updates, and integration additions are all pushed automatically. No recurring fees, no upgrade charges.
