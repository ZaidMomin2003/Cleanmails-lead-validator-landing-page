Add an A Record

Point your domain/subdomain to your VPS

Go to your DNS provider (Cloudflare, Namecheap, GoDaddy, etc.) and add an A record that points to your VPS IP address.

Type Name Value TTL
A verify Your VPS IP Auto
warning

Set the record to DNS only (grey cloud). The orange proxy breaks SSL auto-provisioning.

DNS propagation takes 2–5 minutes. You can proceed to Step 2 while you wait.

Set Reverse DNS (rDNS)


Required for accurate SMTP verification








Mail servers check that your IP's reverse DNS matches your domain. Without this, many SMTP checks will fail or be flagged as suspicious.





How to set it:


    

  1. Go to your VPS provider's dashboard
    2. 

  2. Find "Reverse DNS", "rDNS", or "PTR record"
    3. 

  3. Set your IP's rDNS to your domain:
    
164.92.x.x → verify.yourdomain.com
    4.
